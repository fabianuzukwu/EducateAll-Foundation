THE JOURNEY FROM STREET TO SCHOOL

The journey of Aarohan, an NGO providing free education to underprivileged children in the Indian capital New Delhi, started way back in 2005. Since then, with the help of various individuals and organisations, Aarohan has been able to transform the lives of thousands of children, who would have otherwise become victims of child labour. MIGTV visited the NGO to capture the stories of these children.

One such success story is that of Bhawna, whose life was changed by Aarohan when she joined as a student, and now she teaches in the same institution to change the lives of others.
