Who we are

EducateALL Foundation Nigeria® is a non-profit charitable organization, founded in 2019 to support vulnerable children in need, especially those facing difficult socio-economic challenges in Nigeria.It has been created to provide a safe haven and a loving family environment to those who are young and helpless in the society and to provide them all the needed help including education, health and even to keep their cultures alive. The aim of EducateALL Foundation is purely helping the underprivileged children from disadvantaged backgrounds. Please join hands with us for volunteering, fundraising and other activities to support these children.

Mission Statement

1.To be an equal opportunity provider for the upliftment of underprivileged children by providing them with timely education and care. 2.EducateAll is a non profit created to educate and empower every child in Nigeria to achieve more.

Vision Statement

1. EducateAll envisions a world in which the most vulnerable children will be fully equipped to lift themselves out of poverty and create a better life for themselves, their families and communities. 2. Our Vision is to reach the maximum needy, to be in reach of all the needy, be the most sought for, recognized and admired organisation, supporting the overall development of underprivileged children.

Modus (Our Strategy)

EducateAll operates strictly with grants and donations and we encourage individuals and institutions to give back by providing them the opportunity to positively impact the lives of these children and their communities. Donations are received via the EducateAll Foundation website with future evolutions that will include mobile apps and targeted social media platforms.

Our Core Values

Education: We aim to reduce the number of out-of-school children, one child at a time by educating 5000 children every year for the next 10 years. End illiteracy of children from disadvantaged backgrounds. Accountability: Implement transparent, effective and efficient end to end processes for crowdsourcing charitable giving and donations using the web and financial technology tools and platforms. Integrity: Develop a strong, trust-worthy and lasting relationship with donors, partners and government agencies. Commitment: Provide sufficient training and financial support for families to ensure long term success of the children. Perseverance: Employ Online and Offline Awareness campaigns and stakeholder engagements on the need to promote qualitative education for the country’s future leaders

Your Support: The EducateAll Advantage

The EducateAll platform offers a transparent and convenient medium for charitable giving, where donors can give openly and anonymously. Donors who wish to play a deeper role in the life of recipients they sponsor can also do so in a structured mentoring programme that will include regular access to progress and grade reports, visits and special meetups. All of these processes are technology-driven and managed by dedicated case officers.

Projects

*Annual Backpack Giving The EducateAll Foundation will be providing backpacks with various school supplies to improvised school children. This will be our yearly goal toward assisting the children and their parents and one of the reasons why we need your support. The foundation will work with various organisations, corporations and individuals to ensure we achieve all our goals. This activity would be held at the beginning of the school year so the children are properly equipped when school starts. For this reason, we also accept donations of material goods. These include school bags, school supplies, shoes, etc. *Sponsor a Child Sponsor a Child project creates a unique relationship between you and your sponsored child. One Child for One Year, whether it is donations via school supplies or monetary donations you can make a long-lasting impact and build on a relationship that includes the family and communities.
