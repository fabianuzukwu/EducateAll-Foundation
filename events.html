EVENTS

Recent Events

The Annual Fundraising Program

On the 22nd of March, 2019, we held our 1st annual fundraising program at the Sheraton Hotel and Suites,Lekki, Lagos. This was sponsored by HNG Tech and we raised 6.4 million Naira, which was our biggest raise yet. The funds will be used in re-stocking some public libraries with books, getting at least 400 students enrolled in secondary schools, and so much more.

Fundraising Programme at Abuja

On the 1st of October 2019, we would be holding another fundraising event at our capital city, Abuja. This event would be sponsored by Flutterwave Inc. and our target is to surpass that of Lagos and raise 10 million naira. This event would take place at The Sheraton Hotel in Abuja.

World Education Day Parade

On the 8th of september 2018 thousands joined us as we walked together in the World Education Day Parade. Everyone was invited to join in the parade. Participants were encouraged to wear jeans and sports wear, an EducateAll t-shirt was also be provided.

Upcoming Events

Enlightening the Masses

On the 22nd of September, we are going to rally around some remote places in Lagos, spreading the word about the importance of education. Your presence would be highly appreciated.

Maiden Backpack Giving Day

On the 1st of September, we will have our annual Backpack Giving event. This promises to be so much fun as we will be giving out backpacks and school supplies to the children.

Kids Bake Sales

The upcoming Kids Bake Sale promises to be one of our most anticipated events of the year! The kids will get to interact with community members and increase their sense of inclusion. Also, our donors will get the chance to see some of our success stories. It promises to be loads of fun!
