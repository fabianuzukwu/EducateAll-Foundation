EVENTS

Recent Events

Enlightening the Masses

On the 22nd of September, we are going to rally around some remote places in Lagos, spreading the word about the importance of education. Your presence would be highly appreciated.

Family Get Together (September 2019)

Our family consists of every child that will be helped by EducateAll Foundation and every other person that has made helping them a reality. We gather every last Saturday in the month to share our progress, encourage one another, have fun with one another, and remind ourselves of the reason why we began this foundation in the first place.

Maiden Backpack Giving Day

On the 1st of September, we will have our annual Backpack Giving event. This promises to be so much fun as we will be giving out backpacks and school supplies to the children.

Upcoming Events

Kids Bake Salese

The upcoming Kids Bake Sale promises to be one of our most anticipated events of the year! The kids will get to interact with community members and increase their sense of inclusion. Also, our donors will get the chance to see some of our success stories. It promises to be loads of fun!

World Education Day Parade

Join us as we walk together in the World Education Day Parade. Everyone is invited to join in the parade. Participants are encouraged to wear jeans and sports wear, an EducateAll tshirt will also be provided.

Teach the Inmate

Calling on all teachers, educators and education enthusiasts to be part of this great initiative. The Teach the Inmate program aims to reduce incarceration rates in Nigeria by increasing access to post-secondary school education in prisons.
